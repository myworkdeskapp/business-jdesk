Core features

Everything your HR team needs

From hiring to retiring — WorkDesk handles every step of the employee lifecycle with elegance and precision.

Employee Directory

Centralise all employee records, org charts, and profiles in one searchable directory with role-based access control.

Attendance Tracking

Real-time clock-in/clock-out with GPS verification, shift scheduling, and automated overtime calculations.

Leave Management

Streamline leave requests, approvals, and balances. Full visibility for managers with configurable leave policies.

Payroll Engine

Automated payroll processing with tax calculations, deductions, allowances, and downloadable payslips.

Performance Reviews

Set OKRs, run 360° reviews, track KPIs, and give continuous feedback to foster a high-performance culture.

Recruitment Pipeline

Post jobs, manage applicants, schedule interviews, and collaborate on hiring decisions — all in one kanban board.

How it works

Up and running
in minutes

No complex setup, no IT department required. WorkDesk is designed to be operational the same day you sign up.

1

Create your workspace

Sign up, name your organisation, and invite your HR team. Your workspace is live instantly.

2

Import your employees

Upload a CSV or add employees manually. WorkDesk auto-creates profiles, contracts, and access credentials.

3

Configure your policies

Set leave policies, payroll schedules, attendance rules, and performance cycles to match your company.

4

Run HR on autopilot

Let WorkDesk handle the routine so you focus on your people. Reminders, reports, and payroll run automatically.
